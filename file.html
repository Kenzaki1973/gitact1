About GTA 5

Grand Theft Auto V is an action-adventure game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. Set within the fictional state of San Andreas, based in Southern California, the single-player story follows three protagonists—retired bank robber Michael De Santa, street gangster Franklin Clinton, and drug dealer and arms smuggler Trevor Philips—and their attempts to commit heists while under pressure from a corrupt government agency and powerful crime figures.
